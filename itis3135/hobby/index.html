The Collection Details

Scheduling

Other than Comic Books, which is a once a month pick up. There is no real schedule to the "when" of my collecting procurement. Unless a preorder had been priorly placed, and a purchse is expected.

Procurement

Most things such as the movies, figures, and trading cards are procured online via online shops. But the comic books are acquired throuhg a local comic shop. When it comes to retail comic shops, I've always found it fundmentally important to support your local shops.

How do I Manage So Many Items

Managing such a large assortment of items can be tough, but I have boxes for organizing and stacking comics. A large specialized shelf for managing my physical movie media. Also a large assortment of shelves for managing the figures. Dusting and cleaning the items is important for keeping the value of said items.

Why do I Collect?

Collecting is something I always admired people for doing. It's almost like trophy hunting for things the encapsulate your life and/or childhood.
