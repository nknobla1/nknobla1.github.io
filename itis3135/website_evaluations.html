Website Evalutions

YouTube is an example of a Well Designed Website

One of my most frequented sites, Youtube, is a website for video entertainment and other assorted video content viewing.

  • C - Contrast: Uses black font against a white background, making it easy to read and navigate.
  • R - Repetition: The layout is in a repeatable format for easy of finding specific sections for viewing.
  • A - Alignment: Everything is in a neat column format, with noticable breakage being only for ads.
  • P - Proximity: With it being aligned properly, there isn't much clutter if at all.

ARNGREN is an example of a Terrible Website

ARNGREN is a website whose goal is to sell a variety of technologies to a consumer. But it fails on almost every CRAP design principle.

  • C - Contrast: It uses alot of bright colors against white backgrounds.
  • R - Repetition: It's consistant in its usage of boxing elements, but it isn't uniform in its execution.
  • A - Alignment: Almost nothing outside of the sidebar is uniform in tis alignment.
  • P - Proximity: The whole site is the definition of clutter.
